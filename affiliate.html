What Are Affiliate Links?

Affiliate links are special tracking URLs that allow us to earn a commission when you purchase a product or service after clicking our link. These links help us monetize our content while keeping our guides and resources free for you.

Example: If we write a review about an AI tool and you click our affiliate link to purchase a subscription, we may receive a percentage of the sale as a commission. The price you pay remains the same whether you use our link or not.
